Los Angeles , USA

Day 1

Los Angeles

Welcome to the city of charismatic dreams.Upon your arrival at the USA Airport, you’ll be met and greeted by an agent’s representative who will escort you to the hotel after an exhausting journey. After check in relax and explore the city in your way. Return to the hotel, enjoy the grand buffet followed by the overnight stay.

Day 2

Los Angeles

Enjoy the ultimate sightseeing experience of this ravishing city.Wake up to a healthy breakfast and get charged up for an exciting day. Today you’ll go for a sightseeing tour from Anaheim. The local guide will pick you up at 8:40 am from Anaheim Disneyland area and take you to the Walk of Fame where you’ll see the Dolby theatre, Chinese theatre, and the renowned Hollywood sign.

Day 3

Los Angeles

Visit the world-famous amusement park.After a ravishing breakfast gets ready to visit the Universal Studios Hollywood. The guide will come to pick you up from Anaheim at 10 am. On the relaxing ride to the northwest to Hollywood enjoy with your partner.

Day 4

Los Angeles

Post breakfast, get charged up to visit Catalina Island. You’ll be picked up from the hotel from where you’ll head to the Long Beach Landing Terminal where you’ll board a ferry which will take you to the island. The duration of the journey is 1 hour which will leave you mesmerized till you reach your destination.

Day 5

Los Angeles

After a grand buffet breakfast, you are free to either stay back at the hotel and enjoy its ravishing facilities or explore the city on your own. You can even head to the Disneyland Resort which is only a 15-minute walk from your hotel. Return at night to enjoy the band performance on your last night in the USA.
