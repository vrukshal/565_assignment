Gold Coast & Sydney , Australia

Day 1

Gold Coast

The Gold Coast experience with waters and whales will be exceptional.After your safe landing at Sydney Airport. We will take another direct flight to the Gold Coast. One of our best agents will get you from the Airport and will lead you to the dedicated hotel. He will help you with check-in and will take you to your rooms. After that, you will be free to go anywhere you want.

Day 2

Gold Coast

2nd day will be a fun filling involvement for you. After a delightful breakfast, we will take you on an adventure you would never forget. We will board a whale-watch cruise to look for the humpback whales on the shores of Gold Coast. We will ride a boat that is made with three site views, from where you will have spectacular views of the Pacific and attend to explanation about the whales and their ocean environment from an aquatic environmentalist attendant.

Day 3

Gold Coast

After a lip-smacking breakfast, you will be having a full day of freedom. You will be allowed to go anywhere in the city on your own will. You could request for a cab with a driver and wander around. Spend some dollars and get the full day of fun ahead. You can be out there till late at night.

Day 4

Sydney

Post a luxurious breakfast, we will proceed to check-out from the hotel. Our agent will take you to the Airport for your flight to Sydney. After safe arrival, we will proceed to Sydney hotel and perform all the formalities of check-in. Later in the day, you can enjoy city tours on a private vehicle and enjoy the city of love and attractions fully.

Day 5

Sydney

After a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel, your guide for the day picks you up in the morning and we will spend half day at Sydney morning city tour. Start time: 8.00 am. There will be no hotel drop-offs after this tour. Our central departure point will be Lime Street at King Street Wharf.

Day 6

Sydney

On the 6th morning of our tour, we will check out from the hotel. Our agent will escort you to the Airport. After reaching the airport, board your flight back home. Hope you had a wonderful vacation. The memories we created along with each other will bring you back to us to travel again soon.
